About WeatherWise

Story Behind WeatherWise

The inspiration for WeatherWise came from a personal experience. Last summer, while planning a hiking trip, I realized I wanted to make my own weatherwise weather application with my own design and features. This sparked the idea of creating a user-friendly weather application that could provide precise weather updates for any location, whether urban or remote.

WeatherWise was developed as a part of my Portfolio Project for ALX. The project served as a culmination of my learning and aimed to solve a real-world problem. Working on WeatherWise has been a journey of growth and learning, where I applied skills in web development, API integration, and user experience design.
